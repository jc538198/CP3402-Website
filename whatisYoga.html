About Yoga

Yoga has a long history, has been practiced for more than 5,000 years, and has brought healing powers that benefit generations to come. By practicing yoga to move your body and regulate your breathing, you can mature your mind, express your emotions, and keep your body healthy. But yoga is not just a physical exercise, it can speed up the metabolism, from the inside to the outside of the body conditioning, prevention and elimination of some diseases; Improve people's internal and external temperament, make body light and so on.
